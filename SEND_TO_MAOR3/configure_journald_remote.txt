# =============================
# CIS Section 6.2.1.2 - Configure systemd-journal-remote
# =============================

---
6.2.1.2.1 Ensure systemd-journal-remote is installed
---

Description:
Verify systemd-journal-remote is installed.

Rationale:
Remote log storage protects logs from local tampering.

Expected State:
systemd-journal-remote should be installed.

Current State:
systemd-journal-remote is NOT installed

Audit Result:
FAIL

Remediation:
Run: apt install systemd-journal-remote

---


---
6.2.1.2.3 Ensure systemd-journal-upload is enabled and active
---

Description:
Ensure systemd-journal-upload is enabled and active

Rationale:
Active log upload service ensures forwarding logs to remote host

Expected State:
systemd-journal-upload should be enabled and active

Current State:
Enabled: , Active: inactive

Audit Result:
FAIL

Remediation:
Run: systemctl unmask systemd-journal-upload && systemctl --now enable systemd-journal-upload

---


---
6.2.1.2.4 Ensure systemd-journal-remote service is not in use
---

Description:
Ensure systemd-journal-remote is NOT enabled or active

Rationale:
Clients should not be configured to receive logs

Expected State:
systemd-journal-remote.service and .socket should be disabled/inactive

Current State:
systemd-journal-remote.socket and service are not enabled or active

Audit Result:
PASS

Remediation:
Run: systemctl stop systemd-journal-remote.socket systemd-journal-remote.service && systemctl mask systemd-journal-remote.socket systemd-journal-remote.service

---
